Video Player

The main focus for this project was to create a video player and custom skin for said player. along side the custom skin for this player I have also included a script for the video that highlights as the video plays as well as the ability to click anywhere on the script and to jump to that spot on the video.

Highlights:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • SASS
  • Media Queries
  • JavaScrip
  • Plugin
